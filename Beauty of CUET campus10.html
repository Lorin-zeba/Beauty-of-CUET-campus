CUET is abbreviation of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology.(Bengali:চট্টগ্রাম প্রকৌশল ও প্রযুক্তি বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়)It is a public engineering university in Bangladesh located in Raozan Upozila under Chittagong District.It is called green heaven for its evergreen beauty.
